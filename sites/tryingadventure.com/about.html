What is Trying Adventure

I built this site to support my hobby, photography. I have been involved in photography since I was in college where my first camera was a Pentax K1000. That was the best camera to expose anyone to the basics of photography and the fundementals of light. The K1000 was simple but you really had to know your stuff, especially with film photography. Now we have digital cameras with amazing smarts built in. Even better, everyone has an amazing camera built right into your smartphone. Now everyone takes great photos right? Yes, that is true, but I still choose to lug around a full frame sensor camera because smartphones still have not figured out completely to emulate the physics of a lens in order to maximize create flexibility.

About Trying Adventure

I did what any great engineer does, I built my own site.
